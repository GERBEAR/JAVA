                  13.55 

                  FIVE MINUTES TO KICK-OFF: The players are making their way out onto the pitch and the atmosphere is a friendly one, so far. Let’s hope that continues until well after the match.

                  13.50 

                  TEN MINUTES TO KICK-OFF: Slovakia lost their Euro 2016 opener to Wales but they have not been beaten in back-to-back matches since October 2013.

                  13.45

                  Danger man...

                  -
                  13.40

                  Slovakia to target leaky Russian defence. They have kept just two clean sheets in their last 16 games at the Euro finals, including matches under their former Soviet Union and CIS entities.

                  -
                  -

                  Filling up..

                  13.35

                  This will be the ninth time these two countries have met with Russia claiming three victories to Slovakia's two. Slovakia have scored only one goal in their last four games against Russia and only three goals in total have been scored in the last four meetings between the sides. It could be a tight, tense affair...

                  13.30

                  A quick reminder of both line-ups today.

                  13.25

                  Russia head coach Leonid Slutski (via Uefa): "We need a different tactical approach for the game against Slovakia, than the one used in the game with England. But the main thing, and I’ll say this again, we need changes to how we play. That might depend on the personnel, or on our shape. With England it was very difficult to attack because they were very fast and aggressive but I think we will have more opportunities to attack playing against Slovakia. Wales had periods where they were in control and were attacking so, like I said, this won’t be an easy match.”

                  13.20

                  Ján Kozák, Slovakia coach (via Uefa): "The Russian team is made up of players who play for teams like CSKA Moskva and Zenit, who regularly play in the UEFA Champions League. They have improved under Slutski and are very well organised, with good transition from defence to attack and vice versa. The Wales defeat was unnecessary and when we reviewed it, we saw that the Welsh made more mistakes; we just couldn't take our chances. But we did make mistakes and now we have a chance to make amends against Russia."

                  13.15 

                  Russia midfielder Denis Glushakov is still not fit enough to start following a calf strain having played the final 10 minutes against England. But Ondrej Duda does make the Slovakia starting XI after scoring against the Welsh shortly after coming on a substitute.

                  13.00 

                  Hello and welcome to our LIVE match coverage of this crucial Euro 2016, Group B clash between Russia and Slovakia from the Stade Pierre-Mauroy. Much of the pre-match focus has focused on the uglier side of things, with Russia being threatened with disqualification if there is a repeat of the fan disturbances that marred the opening fixture against England. Let’s hope, however, that there is no repeat and that it is the football that is talked about.
